# 1. Executive Summary

BHome is an AI-powered accommodation marketplace that helps users confidently discover, evaluate, and book short-term and long-term rentals.

Unlike traditional platforms that focus on listing inventory, BHome focuses on helping users find the best property for their lifestyle, budget, safety requirements, travel plans, and personal preferences.

The platform combines:

* AI Matching
* Verified Listings
* 360° Property Experiences
* Community Validation
* Trust Scores
* Gamification
* Location Intelligence
* Rewards Economy

BHome aims to become the most trusted rental platform in the world.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Current rental platforms suffer from several major issues:

### For Renters

* Fake listings
* Misleading photos
* Hidden fees
* Unreliable reviews
* Poor understanding of neighborhoods
* Time wasted browsing unsuitable properties
* No personalized recommendations
* Difficulty comparing locations
* Unclear safety information
* Lack of booking confidence

### For Hosts

* Poor lead quality
* Low trust from renters
* Difficulty standing out
* High marketing costs
* Low repeat bookings
* Lack of meaningful analytics
